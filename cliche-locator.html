About the Cliché Locator

Inspiration

I was inspired to create Cliché Locator during a class I took called "Creative Genius," during my freshman year at Trinity University. Much to my dismay, the site Cliche Finder already existed, but that didn't stop me from reinventing the wheel. Much of Cliché Locator's functionality is modeled after Cliche Finder.
